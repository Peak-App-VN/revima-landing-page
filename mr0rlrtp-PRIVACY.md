# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Revima** ("Revima", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Revima mobile application (the "App"). Revima is an AI-powered photo tool that restores, colorizes, and enhances your photos.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

Revima is designed to work **without requiring you to create an account**. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or social profiles. The information we process falls into the following categories.

### 2.1 Photos You Upload

When you choose to restore, colorize, or enhance a photo, the image you select is uploaded to our servers so that our AI system can process it. The original photo and the generated result are stored so we can show them in your **History** and let you download them.

We process your photos **only** to provide the editing features you request. We do not use your photos to train our models, sell them, or share them for advertising.

### 2.2 Device and Session Information

To create an anonymous session and protect the service from abuse, we collect:

- **Platform** — whether your device is iOS or Android.
- **Device fingerprint** — a stable, app-specific identifier:
  - On **iOS**, a random identifier (UUID) generated on first launch and stored in the device Keychain.
  - On **Android**, the system `ANDROID_ID` value.

This identifier does **not** reveal your real-world identity. It allows us to maintain your session, apply usage limits, and prevent fraud and abuse.

### 2.3 Reports You Submit

If you report a result (for example, "poor image quality" or "inappropriate content"), we collect the **reason** you select and any **optional free-text description** you provide, together with a reference to the affected generation, so we can review and improve the service.

### 2.4 Technical and Diagnostic Data

We may process limited technical data needed to operate the App, such as request identifiers and timestamps, error and crash information, and basic logs used for security and troubleshooting.

## 3. Information We Do **Not** Collect

- We do not collect your name, email, or contact details.
- We do not require account registration or social login.
- We do not access your photo library beyond the specific images you choose to upload or save.
- We do not sell your personal data.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

- Provide the core features: restoring, colorizing, and enhancing your photos.
- Store and display your edit **History** and allow downloads.
- Maintain your anonymous session and authenticate your requests.
- Enforce fair-use and generation limits and prevent abuse, fraud, and security threats.
- Review reports and improve the quality and safety of the service.
- Diagnose technical problems and maintain reliability.

---

## 5. Permissions We Request

The App may ask for the following device permissions. You can grant or deny them, and you can change your choice later in your device Settings:

- **Photos / Storage** — to let you pick a photo to edit and to save a result to your photo library or downloads folder.

If you deny a required permission, some features (such as saving an image) may not work.

---

## 6. How Your Information Is Stored

- **On your device:** your session tokens are kept in secure, encrypted storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore-backed storage). App preferences are kept in standard local storage.
- **On our servers:** uploaded photos, generated results, and related metadata are stored to provide History and download features.

All communication between the App and our servers uses encrypted connections (HTTPS).

---

## 7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share data only to the limited extent needed to run the service:

- **Cloud infrastructure and storage providers** that host our servers and store images on our behalf.
- **AI processing services** used to generate restored, colorized, or enhanced results.

These providers act on our instructions and are bound to protect your data. We do **not** share your photos or identifiers with advertisers or data brokers.

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the service.

---

## 8. Data Retention

- **Photos and results** are retained so they remain available in your History. When you delete a result in the App, we remove it from your History.
- **Reports** are retained as long as needed for safety review and record-keeping.
- **Session identifiers and logs** are retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy.

You may contact us at any time to request deletion of data associated with your session.
